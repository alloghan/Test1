Popular Destinations

Amazon Forest

The Amazon Forest is located in South America and spans over nine countries. It is the largest tropical rainforest in the world.

Denali Mountain

Denali Mountain is located in Alaska, USA. It is the highest peak in North America, standing at 20,310 feet above sea level.

Rhine Falls

Rhine Falls is located on the Rhine River in Switzerland. It is the largest waterfall in Europe, spanning over 492 feet wide and 75 feet tall.
